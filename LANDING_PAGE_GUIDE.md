## Color Scheme

### Primary Colors
- **Blue Gradient**: `from-blue-600 to-blue-700` (buttons, accents)
- **Emerald**: `emerald-600` (secondary accent)
- **Purple**: `purple-600` (tertiary accent)

### Background Colors
- **Light Gradient**: `from-slate-50 via-blue-50 to-slate-100` (main bg)
- **White/Transparent**: `white/50` or `white/80` (cards, header)

### Text Colors
- **Dark**: `slate-900` (headings, primary text)
- **Medium**: `slate-600` (body text)
- **Light**: `slate-300` (borders, light text)

## Interactive Elements

### Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: Gradient blue with scale animation on hover
- **Secondary**: White with blue border, text changes on hover
- **Links**: Text with color transition on hover

### Cards
- **Feature Cards**: White background with border, hover shadow and color change
- **Tech Stack Cards**: White with centered text, simple hover effects
